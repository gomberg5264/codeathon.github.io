Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver high speed internet world wide for better education. We will achieve this mission by partnering with Starlink. By providing internet to communities that cannot afford it we will empower students around the world


How we're doing it

We will be partnering with internet service providers to bring internet to communities that otherwise wouldn't have it. Our partners are lowering they costs for our organization so we can provide internet to more communities.

These partners will providing internet for reduced rates to bring internet to more communities. Partners include:
  • Starlink
  • Spectrum Internet
  • Viasat
  • HughesNet

We will be using donations from the community to give internet services to communities around the world.


Why we're doing it

Our organization seeks to further education around the world by providing fast and reliable internet.


The Benefits

Our project has many benefits including but not limited to:

  • Communities are provided with easier access to information
  • The ability to connect to others around the world
  • New bussiness oportuinities
  • Access to online banking and shopping
